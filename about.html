About

Organizer is an application designed to make your life more organized and productive. It makes it easier to achieve your personal and professional goals with its three main features.

To-Do

Create, organize and complete your tasks to keep track of your work.

Habit Tracker

Identify your daily habits, track your progress with graphs, and achieve your goals.

Notes

A practical note-taking tool to record your important ideas and information.
